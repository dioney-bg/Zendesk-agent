1. AMER LEADER

New Business ICP:

Win Rate: 7%

Public Sector Segment

Expansion ICP:

Win Rate: 31%

Win Rate: 9%
ARR: $1.01M (24% of AMER NB)
Top Use Cases: IT (60%), Multi-Department (34%)
Motion: IT-led but lower conversion. Niche play for HR/Workforce use cases.

🎯 AMER PRIORITY RECOMMENDATION

#1 Segment: Commercial

Why: Best balance of win rates (40% Exp, 12% NB) + strong ARR ($10.55M Exp, $1.51M NB) + product-ready

vs Enterprise: Commercial has higher win rates (40% vs 35% Exp) and no product constraints

#1 Use Case: IT

Why: 40% of Commercial expansion starts with IT, 57% of Commercial NB is IT-led

Expansion Path: IT → Multi-Department → HR/Operations

Motion:

  • Expansion: IT buyer, expand to multi-department (40% of deals)
  • New Business: IT-led prospecting (57% of NB deals)
